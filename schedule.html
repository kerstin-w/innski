Schedule

Day One

On the event weekend, unlimited equipment (skis, snowboards, helmets, goggles, backpacks, avalanche transceivers, etc.) from the leading manufacturers can be put through their paces free of charge and without obligation. The best thing about it: most of the equipment is already from the next season 2023/24 and therefore brand new.

  • Visiting Test area
  • Checking out the newest equipment from top brands
  • Rent equipment and adjust ski bindings etc if necessary
  • Taking cable car up to Nordkette area for some comprehensive testing

Day Two

We grew up here and as locals we are your best partners off the groomed slopes. We'll show you exactly how it's done, provide you with the relevant background knowledge and get you fit so that you can later move safely and confidently in open alpine terrain.

  • Meeting our Mountain Guides
  • Dividing participiants into groups according to your experience
  • Introduction to route planning
  • Guided ski tour to secret spots around Innsbruck

Day Three

Adapted according to the current circumstances and on the move in the field, we give you our experience on the subject of avalanche prevention. What do we do today and why not the other? Where is the best place to ski our planned line and above all why? What do I do if things turn out differently than planned? These and many other questions occupy us every day, and we are happy to share our thoughts with you.

  • Avalanche and safety training in different workshops
  • Avalanche rescue search training
  • Training in snow and avalanche awareness
  • Slope evaluation and orientation in the terrain
