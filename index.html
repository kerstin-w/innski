Innsbruck Freeride Festival

From 03.01.-05.01.2022

Book Now!

Three Days of Fun, Exploring and Testing!

At the InnSki Festvial everything revolves around freeride skiing. In these three days you can explore the area around Innsbruck with our local mountain guides and find absolute secrete spots for freeriding with powder guarantee. We also offer an integrated Snow Safety training with professionals to make sure you are always safe on the slopes. You will also have the opportunity to test the latest and most innovative material from top brands. And all this in a great group of likeminded people.

What you can expect

Guiding

Together with mountain guides you will discover the best spots around Innsbruck that you would never find otherwise. With their knowledge our mountain guides will find the best powder spots for any conditions.

Test Center

Test the latest and most innovative material from top brands. We have it all: skis, avalanche backpacks and outerwear.

Snow Safety

Stay Safe: Take part in avalanche training to save your buddies quickly in the worst case, but above all to avoid getting into this situation in the first place and learn to assess the risks properly.
